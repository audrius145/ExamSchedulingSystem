FEELING STRESSED OR ANXIOUS ABOUT EXAMS?

Exam anxiety is a problem among students, but at VIA Horsens, you can find someone to talk with and get advice on how you can handle it. Make an appointment with the VIA Student Chaplain and Psychotherapist, Annette Foged Schultz at afs@via.dk.

Look After Your Mental Wellbeing During Exams

Looking after your mental wellbeing is super important at all times, but especially during the exam period...

...we've got some tips on how to look after yourself during this stressful time.



Form Good Habits

Eat Well
Eat foods like bread, rice, pasta, fruit and vegetabels to help maintian your energy.
Drink Plenty of Water
Drinking plenty of water can help with your energy and focus levels.
Keep Active
Exercising is a great way to de-stress and clear your mind, allowing you to focus on your learning.
Get Rest
Good sleep is essential for good mental and physical health, so try to get enough rest while you are studying.

Take Breaks

It can be tempting to focus only on your studies around exam time...

but you shouldn't exclude family and friends from your life!

  • Studying 24/7 isn't productive
  • Take regular short breaks
  • Taking planned breaks is nothing to feel guilty about
